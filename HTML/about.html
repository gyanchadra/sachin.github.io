About AI Resume Analyzer

AI Resume Analyzer is a smart web application designed to help students and job seekers create professional resumes and improve them using AI-based analysis.

Many users struggle to create a proper resume. This platform makes it easy by guiding users and providing suggestions to improve their resume quality.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help users present their skills in the best way and increase their chances of getting selected in jobs.

What We Offer

✔ Easy Resume Builder
✔ Resume Analysis (ATS Based)
✔ Skill Suggestions
✔ Score Prediction

Why Choose Us

Our platform is simple, fast, and beginner-friendly. It is specially designed for students and freshers who want to build strong resumes without confusion.
